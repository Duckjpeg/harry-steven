    extra info

  • +£5 for a layer of wax to be applied after cleaning (optional)

    outside cleaning tiers

  • basic outside - windows cleaned, bodywork shampoo'd and polished
  • premium outside - basic tier + free wax application, tire cleaning and a deeper wheel cleaning

    inside cleaning tiers

  • basic inside - all seats and matts hoovered and plastic surfaces cleaned (dirt / mud may not be fully removed from plastic surfaces at this level)
  • premium inside - basic tier + all seats or floor matts cleaned with a carpet cleaner (removes mud and spill marks). also all plastic surfaces are cleaned to a higher level removing any dirt mud or dust from them.
    for cars with leather seats the seats are cleaned and a protective leather balm is applied, (deep matt and floor clean not included for leather cars at this tier)
  • deuluxe inside - premium tier + all interior shampoo'd. matts, seats and floor cleaned with a carpet cleaner.
    restores to near if not show room level. for leather cars all floor matts and flooring is shampoo'd and cleaned with a carpet cleaner and the leather balm is also still applied.
